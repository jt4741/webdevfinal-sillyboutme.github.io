what is this?

Basically, this website is my way of expressing myself. It is a way for me to share some of my work but also just share different thoughts I have and things I like. I hope spending some time here has been able to bring you some sort of inspiration or learn something and love something you may have not before. As it comes to layout and style, I wanted to make this the most me, in your face, unapologetically myself kind of website. I think most professional websites are so focused on the user perceiving their website, but this has been designed with no focus on how you percieve me. With this in mind, I hope you can view this site with a different lens, one that might even challenge you to be you without fear of what others might think.
