win or lose

It didn’t feel right to make a collection referencing my time at NYU and in New York and not mention NYU WOmen’s soccer. I originally committed to come here and for me NYI Women’s soccer was my path to NYU and out of my last environment. I learned to trust in a healthier environment and to meet new people. NYU helped me realize that soccer is so much more than just a sport or a game that in the right environment it can be a support system and a community. It has helped me to create life long friendships and taught me so many lessons.
