good nite

I wanted to create something as an ode to NYC night life. Before New York, parties started and ended by 11:00 and here they have not even begun yet. This culture is so unique and so exciting. I tried my best to encapsulate this feeling of youth and excitement, adventure and mystery into this piece. I constantly wanted to spoof on this idea of having a “good” night and the phrase goodnight that you say when you go to bed. Stylistically, I wanted it to feel like a piece of my journal or something girly and young that you could feel when you looked at.
