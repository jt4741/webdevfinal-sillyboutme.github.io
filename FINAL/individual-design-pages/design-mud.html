drink a cup of coffee

I lived outside this coffeeshop, “MUD” when I first moved to NYC. It holds so man memories and challenges and struggles. It was simply one of the most beautiful times. I grew up so much in this tiny apartment and because of that it will be one of my most important places in New York. I juxtaposed it with the graffiti that says “I fell for myself this time,” which for me perfectly encapsulates me coming to New York for myself and on my own. It represents for me in a way the start of me learning self love and self resilience.
