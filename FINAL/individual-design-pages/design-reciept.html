How much $$$$$$

I wanted to create an ode to all the reasons why I came to NYU and the city. But all the reasons that made me come here are not the same reasons that have made me stay. So I felt like th best way to show that was through a mock up of a receipt. I priced all of the “products” at $0 because to me they are priceless. However, they all add up to a great deal of money. This represents the wealth of love, knowledge and passion I have from the city. I also felt it would e fitting to use a receipts as NYC is one of the most expensive cities in the world and for some they find it ridiculous but there is a part of me that understands the value of it.
