Design Lab Collection

Reflection

In class I was tasked with creating a project completely free of boundaries or rubrics, so I thought it would be most fitting to share it with you here on this website. These are a collection of 9 prints, all created with Adobe Suite. They represent my experiences and revelations as I have moved to New York City and how it has symbolically changed my life. Despite being presented as a collection, all of these prints are designed to stand alone, they were not designed to aesthetically belong together, but belong together via hidden meanings, motifs, and above all the theme of growing and learning in New York City. There are many hidden clues and meanings and I challenge you to draw connections and try your best to decipher the art and the meaning.
