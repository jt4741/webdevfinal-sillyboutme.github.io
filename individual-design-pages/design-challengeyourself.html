Challenge Yourself

I created ‘challenge yourself’ based on a photo I took at a coffee shop in Brooklyn. I don’t consider myself a photographer but this is one of the most special photos I have ever taken. This photo documented two strangers meeting up at a local coffee shop to play a game of checkers. Something that seems so simple created such a lasting effect on me. Before New York, I found myself crippled with social anxiety and the thought of collaborating and meeting a stranger felt so foreign to me. As I have grown up and continue to live in the city I feel myself begin to shift. Things that once held me in such crippling anxiety have set me free and allowed me to flourish. It hasn’t been easy but it has been real and that is why I added the words “challenge yourself” to the image. To represent how NY has challenged me, how I have had to challenge myself in order to grow and evolve.
